About the Author

R.A. Crawford is the author of the epic fantasy series The Raven's Heir Trilogy.

Inspired by mythology, history, loyalty, betrayal, and the shadows between, R.A. crafts stories of power, legacy, and the choices that shape worlds.

When not writing, he can be found building, creating, or exploring the realms of stories in every form.
