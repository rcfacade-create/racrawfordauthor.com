About R.A. Crawford

R.A. Crawford is the author of the epic fantasy series The Raven's Heir Trilogy.

Inspired by mythology, history, ancient houses, hidden bloodlines and the shadows between, R.A. Crawford crafts stories of power, legacy, loyalty, betrayal and the choices that shape worlds.

When not writing, he can be found building, creating, exploring stories in every form, and developing the world behind The War of Feather and Shadow.

I didn't want to just tell you one story, I wanted to invite you to a new world where stories are made, legends are born and legacy's created.
