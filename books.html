About the Author

Meet R.A. Crawford, author of epic fantasy and creator of The Raven’s Heir Trilogy.

R.A. Crawford writes epic fantasy shaped by myth, history, political intrigue and the shadows between power and legacy.

His work explores loyalty, betrayal, inheritance, sacrifice and the choices that shape worlds.

When not writing, he can be found building, creating, sketching, and exploring the realms of stories in every form.

I didn’t want to just tell you one story. I wanted to invite you to a new world where stories are made, legends are born and legacies are created.
