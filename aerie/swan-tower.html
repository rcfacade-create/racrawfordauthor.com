Archive Record: Swan Tower

Swan Tower is the Academy’s centre of diplomacy, courtly discipline and ceremonial instruction. Its students are trained in speech, restraint, negotiation and the quiet manipulation of power.

The Swans understand that a blade may win a duel, but a well-placed word can end a war before it begins.

Certain Swan diplomatic records remain sealed by House authority.
