The Great Library

The Hall of Chronicles is the Academy’s formal archive and great library. Its galleries rise through shadowed levels of parchment, locked cabinets, ancient ledgers and owl-marked reading chambers.

House Owl preserves the records within, though preservation does not always mean permission. Some shelves are open to students. Others require House authority, bloodline clearance, or a name already written in the archive itself.

Deep archive access remains restricted.
