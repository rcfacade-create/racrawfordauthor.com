The Academy as Design

This master blueprint records the known structure of Aethelmar Academy: the Great Courtyard, the House Towers, the Hall of Chronicles, the Great Hall, and the sealed Raven wing.

Later scholars believe the Academy was never designed merely as a place of learning. Its architecture appears to guide, test and conceal — as though stone, timber and tower were arranged around a purpose older than the Dominion itself.

Several structural annotations remain sealed.
