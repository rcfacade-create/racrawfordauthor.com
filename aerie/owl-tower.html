Archive Record: Owl Tower

Owl Tower is devoted to memory, learning and the preservation of ancient knowledge. Its students are trained to read not only ink and parchment, but silence, pattern and omission.

The Owls steward the Hall of Chronicles and maintain the Academy’s deepest records. Not all shelves are open to students. Not all doors admit the living.

Certain Owl records remain sealed beneath archival authority.
