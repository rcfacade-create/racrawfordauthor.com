Archive Record: Crow Tower

Crow Tower is home to those trained in silence, perception and hidden knowledge. Its halls are narrow, its rooms watchful, and its lessons rarely begin where students expect them to.

Master Grey once taught within these chambers, shaping Crow students through observation, misdirection and carefully measured truth.

Certain Crow records remain withheld by order of the Royal Archive.
