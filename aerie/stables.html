Aethelmar Stables

The Academy stables house the Tianma — rare bird-like flying horses used for travel, ceremony and specialist training.

Archive Record

The Tianma Grounds

Set away from the busiest Academy paths, the stables are built with high open roofs, reinforced perches, wind galleries and enclosed flight yards. The Tianma are not ordinary mounts. They are intelligent, proud and difficult to command.

Students permitted near the stables are taught patience before riding, trust before control, and respect before ambition. A Tianma does not carry a rider it has not judged worthy.

Breeding records and older flight routes remain sealed.
