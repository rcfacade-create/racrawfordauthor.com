Archive Record: Hawk Tower

Hawk Tower trains students in combat, discipline and command. Its balconies overlook the military grounds, where drills, weapons practice and war games shape the Academy’s most battle-ready students.

The Hawks believe strength is not merely force, but control — the ability to strike only when the moment is right.
