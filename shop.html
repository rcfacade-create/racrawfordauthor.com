Keeper of the Crow Stores

Meet Hemming

Hidden behind shelves and secret doors, Hemming’s shop is one of Aethelmar’s most curious places. A small man in Crow leathers, with an eyeglass, a waist-length black beard flecked with grey, and one violet eye, Hemming keeps stock for those who know where to look.

“First one’s on Master Grey. Next one, you’ll have to pay for.”
