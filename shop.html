Proprietor

Hemming

Very small, sharp-eyed, and dressed in House Crow leathers, Hemming keeps his shop hidden behind shifting bookcases and secret doors. One violet eye watches every coin, every blade, and every bargain.

The shop itself moves like a sliding puzzle: walls of weapons, cloaks, masks, gauntlets, tools, rope, instruments, jewellery, bags, packs, scrolls, books, and stands of armour appearing where only shelves once stood.

“First one’s on Master Grey. Next one, you’ll have to pay for.”
