Meet the Author

R.A. Crawford writes immersive fantasy filled with ancient houses, dangerous power, political intrigue, and characters forced to choose between loyalty and destiny.

His debut fantasy novel, The War of Feather and Shadow, opens The Raven Cycle, a world of divided crowns, rising bloodlines, and a war that will reshape everything.
