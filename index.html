Meet the Author

R.A. Crawford

R.A. Crawford writes immersive fantasy filled with ancient kingdoms, political intrigue, unforgettable characters, and worlds where every choice carries a cost.

Inspired by mythology, history, and the timeless struggle between light and shadow, his stories explore loyalty, sacrifice, bloodline, and the price of power.
