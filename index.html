Meet the Author

R.A. Crawford

R.A. Crawford writes fantasy filled with ancient kingdoms, political intrigue, unforgettable characters, and worlds where light and shadow are never as simple as they seem.

Inspired by mythology, history, and the enduring power of storytelling, Crawford creates tales of loyalty, sacrifice, destiny, and the price of power.
